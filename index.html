About Autill

Autill offers innovative autonomous checkout technology with advanced vision technology. Our systems enable smart stores and smart checkouts around the clock – 24/7 – providing a seamless shopping experience without traditional cash registers.

Our Technology

Using modern camera systems, AI-powered image recognition, and real-time processing, our systems capture all movements and purchases in the store. This completely eliminates the checkout process.

Applications

  • 24/7 smart stores in grocery retail
  • Cashier-less gas stations and shops
  • Autonomous shops in airports, train stations, or campuses
  • Pop-up stores and mobile retail spaces

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Autill’s technology work?
Our systems use AI-based camera technology to capture all movements and product removals in the store in real time. This way we automatically know which products were purchased – completely without a traditional checkout process.

What prerequisites does my store need?
Our solutions can be flexibly adapted to existing store layouts. We are happy to discuss feasibility with you in an initial consultation.

How secure are autonomous smart stores?
We rely on GDPR-compliant processing, state-of-the-art security mechanisms, and clearly defined access systems to ensure a safe shopping experience.

How does collaboration work?
After an individual needs analysis, you will receive a tailored offer. Implementation takes place modularly, accompanied by our project team.
